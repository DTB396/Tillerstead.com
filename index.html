Our Expertise

South Jersey Tile & Remodeling Services

From custom tile showers to complete bathroom renovations, each project is approached with a performance-first mindset—focused on proper preparation, standards-based methods, and clean execution built to last.

Tile Installation & Systems

Large-format tile, detailed mosaics, and heated floor systems installed using TCNA-recognized methods. Work emphasizes proper substrate preparation, appropriate trowel selection, coverage verification during installation, and integration with waterproofing assemblies where required.

Learn More

Bathroom Remodeling

End-to-end bathroom renovations, from layout refinement through final fixtures. Projects are planned for functionality, accessibility, and durability, executed in compliance with New Jersey code requirements and backed by Tillerstead's written workmanship warranty.

Learn More

Waterproofing & Leak Mitigation

Shower and wet-area waterproofing assemblies constructed in accordance with ANSI A118.10 standards, with controlled flood testing performed where appropriate. Focused on addressing root causes—substrate conditions, transitions, and detailing—rather than surface-level patching.

Learn More

Substrate Preparation & Structural Readiness

Surface evaluation and preparation for tile and stone installations, including flatness correction, underlayment systems, uncoupling membranes, and deflection review where applicable. Ensures finishes are supported by assemblies designed to perform long term.
