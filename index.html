Questions Answered

Why Our Methods Work Best for NJ Homes

Our approach is tailored for New Jersey: we use TCNA and ANSI standards because they deliver lasting results in our climate and building types.

Do you build full shower waterproofing systems or just tile?

We build complete wet-area assemblies—prep, waterproofing, drains/transitions, then tile—so performance isn't dependent on "surface fixes."

Do you do flood testing?

When the project scope and site conditions allow, flood testing is used to verify the pan/assembly before tile goes in.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve homeowners throughout Atlantic, Ocean, and Cape May Counties—right here in southern New Jersey.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are a licensed New Jersey Home Improvement Contractor (HIC #13VH10808800) with full liability insurance coverage.
