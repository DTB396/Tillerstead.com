Tell Us About Your Project

The fastest way to get an accurate estimate is a clear scope and a few photos. We specialize in tile installations and shower/wet-area systems built as complete assemblies—planned, verified, and documented so your project holds up over time.

Peace of Mind for NJ Homeowners & Businesses. We stand by our builds proudly. Every project follows TCNA standards, New Jersey HIC code, and our rigorous Build Phase methodology—so you know exactly what's happening and why it will last.

What Happens Next

  1. Quick scope review: We review your details and photos and confirm whether we’re a fit.
  2. Clarifying call or text: We confirm priorities, constraints, and scheduling.
  3. Written proposal: You receive a written scope, pricing, and key build assumptions. Any changes are handled through written change orders.

What You Can Expect

  • Written scope: clear inclusions/exclusions, timeline approach, and payment terms
  • System-first planning: prep, waterproofing, and movement accommodation addressed early
  • Verification before finishes: readiness checks before tile conceals the work
  • Documentation: photo record of key phases (useful for maintenance and resale)

What to Send (Helps Us Price Accurately)

  • Photos: wide shots of the room + close-ups of the shower/tub area and any damage
  • Measurements: room size and shower/tub dimensions (rough is fine)
  • Materials: tile size/type and whether you’ve already purchased it
  • Timeline: ideal start window + any deadline constraints
  • Scope notes: “keep layout” vs “reconfigure,” curbed vs curbless, bench/niche plans

Not sure? Send what you have—photos and a short description are enough to start.
