Payment Options for Tile & Bath Projects

Long-lasting tile work depends on proper prep, correct system selection, and disciplined sequencing. If budget is a constraint, we structure scope in phases or you can explore third-party financing—without skipping steps that cause failures later.

Every project starts with a written scope and a clear plan. You’ll know work begins. For tile work, our installation approach references TCNA requirements for the chosen products and systems.

Your Options

  • Pay in full: Ideal for smaller scopes or fast turnarounds.
  • Milestone-based payments: Deposit + progress payments tied to verifiable completion steps (defined in writing).
  • Phase the project: Prioritize what matters most now (e.g., waterproofing + shower, then floors/finishes later).
  • Third-party financing: Apply through Acorn Finance to view offers from multiple lenders (if qualified).

How It Works

  1. Scope & plan: On-site review, measurements, and a written scope. If phasing makes sense, we outline phases.
  2. Written proposal: Line-item estimate and clear terms (scope boundaries, schedule assumptions, and change-order process).
  3. Choose payment method: Pay in full, milestone payments, or apply for third-party financing through Acorn Finance.
