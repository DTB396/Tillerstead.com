How We Work

A documented, code-aware process—so your tile doesn’t become a redo

Written scope, verified prep, waterproofing you can prove, and clean closeout documentation.

  1. 1

    On-site assessment & substrate verification

    A licensed, documented walkthrough focused on real conditions: structure, flatness, moisture risk, and layout feasibility. We verify what matters before tile gets ordered—so the plan is based on your space, not assumptions.

  2. 2

    Written scope, materials, and contract terms

    You receive a written scope that calls out the assembly approach, material categories, and a clear change-order process. Contract terms follow New Jersey HIC requirements (including required notices and cancellation rights).

  3. 3

    Installation, verification, and closeout packet

    Work is executed with jobsite cleanliness and photo documentation (prep → waterproofing details → setting). Flood testing is performed where applicable. You get a closeout packet: care notes, warranty terms, and photo records for future trades and peace of mind.
