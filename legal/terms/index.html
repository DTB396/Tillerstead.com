Terms & Conditions

Last updated: December 2025

1. Licensed & Insured New Jersey Contractor

Tillerstead LLC (“we,” “our,” “us”) is a New Jersey–licensed and insured Home Improvement Contractor (New Jersey HIC License #13VH10808800). All services strictly adhere to the New Jersey Consumer Fraud Act, Home Improvement Practices (N.J.A.C. 13:45A-16), and TCNA 2024 standards for tile and remodeling.

2. Estimates, Proposals & Written Agreements

  • Initial estimates are non-binding and based on site conditions and client-supplied information.
  • Formal proposals specify scope, materials, start dates, pricing, and payment schedules per N.J.A.C. 13:45A-16.2.
  • No work begins until written acceptance (including email) and deposit are received.
  • Proposals remain valid for 30 days unless otherwise stated.

3. Mandatory New Jersey Disclosures

All agreements, as required by N.J.A.C. 13:45A-16, include:

  • Contractor’s legal name, address, phone, and HIC license number.
  • Detailed work description and material specifications.
  • Total price or transparent pricing formula.
  • Start and completion dates or estimated timeline.
  • Statement of the customer’s right to cancel (see below).

Right to Cancel (Three-Day Rule)

For eligible in-home sales, clients may cancel within three business days of contract signing. Written cancellation instructions are provided with every formal agreement.

4. Scheduling & Project Timeline

  • Scheduling is confirmed only after written acceptance and deposit.
  • Timelines may be affected by weather, permitting, material lead times, or unforeseen site conditions.
  • All schedule changes are communicated promptly and transparently.

5. Deposits & Payment Terms

  • Deposits secure scheduling and material procurement.
  • Payment schedules are defined in the written agreement and comply with N.J.A.C. standards.
  • Payments may be milestone-based, progress-based, or tied to material orders.
  • Non-payment may result in work suspension until resolved.

6. Change Orders (N.J.A.C. 13:45A-16.2 Requirement)

Any change to scope, materials, pricing, or timeline requires a written, mutually signed change order. This ensures transparency and protects both client and contractor from misunderstandings.

7. Workmanship & Materials Standards

  • All work is performed to TCNA 2024 standards and manufacturer specifications.
  • Material variations may occur due to manufacturing tolerances.
  • Client-supplied materials must meet project requirements and are installed at client’s risk.

8. Permits, Site Access & Hidden Conditions

  • Clients must provide safe, unobstructed access to the work area.
  • Permit responsibility is defined in the agreement; Tillerstead can obtain permits as required.
  • Discovery of hidden defects (e.g., rot, mold, structural issues, prior improper work) may require additional repairs not included in the initial estimate.

9. Warranties

  • Tillerstead LLC warrants its workmanship per written agreement; warranty applies only to labor performed by Tillerstead.
  • Product warranties are provided by manufacturers and governed by their terms.
  • Damage from misuse, neglect, third-party alterations, or pre-existing conditions is excluded.

10. Cancellations & Refunds

  • Clients may cancel in writing as outlined in the agreement.
  • Deposits may be non-refundable if materials have been ordered or work has commenced, in accordance with N.J.A.C. and contract terms.

11. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by New Jersey law, Tillerstead’s liability is limited to the cost of services rendered. We are not liable for consequential or incidental damages.

12. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

These Terms are governed by New Jersey law. Disputes are resolved in the appropriate New Jersey court with jurisdiction over the project location.
